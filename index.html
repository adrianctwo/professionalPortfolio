eSports Betting
This website is created with the intent to make esports more engaging for the viewers. Players are able to see all upcoming, current, and past League of Legends events. When clicked into each event, every match for that event will be displayed on the screen. Viewers can click into each match and be able to see the live stream or recordings of the match. Viewers can also bet on the match.
eBetz Main Page

The main focus of the page is the live stream and live chat with those who are also watching that stream. Viewers can scroll down for a break down of the two teams that's playing for that match. Furthermore, viewers can also bet on the teams with a simple click on a button.

Fuze
Fuze is a website for Overwatch players who would like to meet new players and form teams to play with. Within the website, players can also check each other's stats and see if they would like to team up on the game.
Fuze Home Page

Train Scheduler
a train schedule application that incorporates Firebase to host arrival and departure data. The app will retrieve and manipulate this information with Moment.js. This website will provide up-to-date information about various trains, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station.
Train Scheduler Home Page

User will input train name, destination, First Train Time, Frequency. This app to calculate when the next train will arrive; this should be relative to the current time. Users from many different machines are able to view same train times since data is being stored in firebase.

Train Scheduler
Train Scheduler Code Snippet

Train Scheduler is built with Firebase, Moment.js, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and CSS. Data is stored in firebase and website populates from data that is stored in firebase. By using firebase, users are able to see train information from different devices.
